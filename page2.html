Thursday, March 12th, 2015

Last class, we learnt how to make buttons on Fireworks.
Here are so images of that:

In order to make a button on Fireworks, you first need to create a rectangular box by using the vector tool. Put a text box inside, select the whole thing, right click to Convert To Symbol.. A new window will pop up, select "button." After you've done that, Fireworks now knows that that is a button. To edit how the button will look when you hoover over it, click it, or release it after clicking it, just double click your button. If that doesn't work, then go to the side navigation bar, click on to document library, there you will see your button, and double click that.
